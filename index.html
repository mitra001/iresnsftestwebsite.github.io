Program Overview

This NSF-funded International Research Experiences for Students (IRES) program provides U.S. students with hands-on international research opportunities that merge Artificial Intelligence (AI), Physics Simulation, and Design Thinking.

Participants collaborate with research mentors from Japan and the U.S. to work on frontier areas such as soft-body simulations, AI-enhanced modeling, and interactive design systems. The program aims to develop globally-minded researchers who can apply computational intelligence to physical systems and creative design challenges.

NSF IRES: Physical AI Design

International Research Experiences for Students — AI and Physics Integration

About the Program

This NSF-funded IRES program provides U.S. students with international research experience in Japan, focusing on the integration of AI and physical simulation for innovative scientific discovery.

Research Areas

  • AI-driven physical simulation (fluids, cloth, rigid and soft bodies)
  • Interactive computational design systems
  • Embodied AI for real-world physics problems

How to Apply

Applications open annually for summer research in Japan. Selected participants collaborate with U.S. and Japanese mentors on cutting-edge research projects.
